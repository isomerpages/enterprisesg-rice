**Prescribed Stockpile Quantity**

|  | White Rice | Parboiled, Ponni and Basmati Rice |
| -------- | -------- | -------- |
| **Prescribed Stockpile Quantity** | 2 times of MIQ  | *2 times of average of 6 months' import quantity* or *5 MT*, whichever is higher |
